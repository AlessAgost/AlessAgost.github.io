The story of the founder

March 11th 1967

The young Alessandro had a rough childhood. He grew in downtown Detroit. He was a homeless and parentless child. He lived off of leftover foods he found in trash can. One day he found a shoe. He took it apart and learnt how to make them. He made a stand where he sold his own shoes and made money. Bit by bit he bought stores and employed lots of people to start the greatest shoe company in the Universe.
